# Tian Fu (天府星) — The Treasury Star

**天府星** (Tian Fu, "Heavenly Treasury") is one of the 14 main stars in Zi Wei Dou Shu. Known as the "Finance Minister" to Zi Wei's "Emperor," Tian Fu governs storage, accumulation, wealth preservation, and financial wisdom.

## Essential Nature

| Attribute | Quality |
|-----------|---------|
| **Element** | Earth (土) |
| **Yin/Yang** | Yang |
| **Category** | Southern Dipper Star (南斗星) |
| **Symbol** | Treasury, granary, safe, vault |
| **Keyword** | Accumulation |

## Personality Profile

Tian Fu individuals are:
- **Prudent and conservative** — they prefer safety over risk
- **Excellent savers** — natural financial accumulators
- **Dignified and composed** — carry themselves with authority
- **Reliable** — others trust them with money and secrets
- **Strategic thinkers** — plan carefully before acting

### Strengths
- Financial acumen and wealth management
- Reliability and trustworthiness
- Organizational skills
- Patience and persistence

### Challenges
- Can be overly cautious or resistant to change
- May miss opportunities due to conservatism
- Tendency toward materialism
- Difficulty with risk-taking

## Career & Wealth

### Ideal Careers
- Banking and finance
- Real estate management
- Accounting and auditing
- Government administration
- Museum or archive curation
- Insurance industry

### Financial Pattern
Tian Fu is one of the most financially stable stars. Wealth tends to accumulate steadily rather than come in sudden windfalls. These individuals are more likely to build wealth through savings and wise investments than through speculation.

## In the 12 Palaces

| Palace | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Life Palace** | Stable, reliable personality; good financial instincts |
| **Career Palace** | Success in structured, hierarchical organizations |
| **Wealth Palace** | Excellent — natural wealth accumulator |
| **Spouse Palace** | Seeks stable, financially secure partner |
| **Migration Palace** | Success in familiar territory; travel for business |

## Key Combinations

- **Tian Fu + Zi Wei**: Exceptional authority and leadership combined with financial wisdom
- **Tian Fu + Wu Qu**: Powerful wealth combination — both financial stars aligned
- **Tian Fu + Tan Lang**: Tension between accumulation and desire — needs balance
- **Tian Fu + Tian Xiang**: Ministerial combination — excellent for government careers

## Historical Note

In ancient China, 天府 was associated with the imperial granary — the storehouse that ensured the empire's survival through famine and war. This star represents the wisdom of preparation and the power of reserves.
