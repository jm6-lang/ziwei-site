# Tian Ji (天机) - The Strategy Star

**Tian Ji** (天机), known as the "Strategy Star" or "Mechanical Star," is one of the six primary stars in Zi Wei Dou Shu. It represents intelligence, strategy, planning, and adaptability.

## Basic Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Chinese Name** | 天机星 |
| **Meaning** | Heavenly Mechanism / Strategy |
| **Element** | Wood |
| **Nature** | Yang |
| **Location** | The brain of the constellation |

## Core Characteristics

People with Tian Ji prominently placed tend to be:

### Strengths
- **Intelligent and Quick-thinking** - Natural learners who grasp concepts quickly
- **Strategic Planners** - Excel at making detailed plans
- **Adaptable** - Can adjust to changing circumstances
- **Curious Minds** - Always seeking new knowledge
- **Problem Solvers** - Good at finding creative solutions

### Challenges
- **Indecisive** - Too many options can lead to paralysis
- **Restless** - May struggle to stay focused
- **Over-thinking** - Can get caught in analysis
- **Sensitive** - May take things too personally

## Star Combinations

### With Positive Stars
- **Tian Ji + Tian Tong** → Excellent for creative enterprises
- **Tian Ji + Wu Qu** → Strong for business and finance
- **Tian Ji + Zi Wei** → Leadership with strategic thinking

### With Challenging Stars
- **Tian Ji + Hua Xue (火星/铃星)** → May indicate volatility
- **Tian Ji + Empty Star** → Can cause insecurity

## Lifelong Luck Cycles

For those with Tian Ji as a main star, favorable periods include:
- Years when strategic planning is key
- Periods for education and skill development
- Times for business planning and restructuring

## Summary

Tian Ji represents the **power of intelligence and strategy**. It bestows:
- Sharp minds and quick thinking
- Strategic planning abilities
- Adaptability and flexibility
- Problem-solving skills

Those influenced by Tian Ji should focus on developing their intellectual gifts while learning to balance analysis with decisive action.
