# Tan Lang (贪狼星) — The Greedy Wolf Star

**贪狼星** (Tan Lang, "Greedy Wolf") is one of the 14 main stars in Zi Wei Dou Shu. The most complex and multifaceted star, Tan Lang governs desire, talent, romance, spirituality, and transformation. It is both the star of indulgence and the star of spiritual awakening.

## Essential Nature

| Attribute | Quality |
|-----------|---------|
| **Element** | Water/Wood (水木) |
| **Yin/Yang** | Yang |
| **Category** | Northern Dipper Star (北斗星) |
| **Symbol** | Wolf, desire, talent, transformation |
| **Keyword** | Desire |

## Personality Profile

Tan Lang individuals are:
- **Multi-talented** — excel in many different fields
- **Charismatic and magnetic** — naturally attract others
- **Desire-driven** — pursue what they want with intensity
- **Transformative** — capable of radical personal change
- **Complex** — embody contradictions (spiritual yet material)

### The Two Paths of Tan Lang

**Material Path (浊):** Indulgence, pleasure-seeking, gambling, affairs, addiction
**Spiritual Path (清):** Religious devotion, artistic mastery, teaching, healing

The same Tan Lang energy that drives worldly desire can, through suffering and transformation, become the foundation of spiritual wisdom.

### Strengths
- Versatility and adaptability
- Charisma and social skills
- Creative talent
- Capacity for reinvention

### Challenges
- Restlessness and dissatisfaction
- Tendency toward excess
- Difficulty maintaining focus
- Jealousy and possessiveness

## Career & Wealth

### Ideal Careers
- Entertainment and performing arts
- Sales and marketing
- Religion and spiritual teaching
- Art and design
- Politics
- Investment and speculation

### Financial Pattern
Tan Lang's wealth pattern is volatile — periods of abundance followed by periods of loss. Gambling and speculative investments appeal to Tan Lang, but disciplined investing is wiser. The key is transforming desire into productive creative energy.

## In the 12 Palaces

| Palace | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Life Palace** | Charismatic, talented, complex personality |
| **Career Palace** | Success in creative, entertainment, or spiritual fields |
| **Wealth Palace** | Volatile — big gains and losses; needs discipline |
| **Spouse Palace** | Passionate but turbulent relationships |
| **Migration Palace** | Charisma shines in social settings |

## Key Combinations

- **Tan Lang + Zi Wei**: "Peach Blossom Emperor" — great authority with romantic appeal
- **Tan Lang + Wu Qu**: "Armed Romance" — military or business success with charisma
- **Tan Lang + Lian Zhen**: "Double Peach Blossom" — extreme romantic/creative energy
- **Tan Lang + Qing Yang / Tuo Luo**: Transformation through suffering → spiritual growth
- **Tan Lang + Huo Xing / Ling Xing**: Sudden explosive success (or crisis)

## The Wolf's Transformation

In Chinese mythology, the wolf that learns to control its hunger becomes the guardian of the mountain. Similarly, Tan Lang individuals who master their desires often become the wisest teachers and most compelling leaders.
