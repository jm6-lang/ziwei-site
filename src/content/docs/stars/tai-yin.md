# Tai Yin (太阴星) — The Moon Star

**太阴星** (Tai Yin, "Great Yin") is one of the 14 main stars in Zi Wei Dou Shu. As the lunar counterpart to Tai Yang (Sun Star), Tai Yin governs femininity, intuition, wealth through property, and emotional intelligence.

## Essential Nature

| Attribute | Quality |
|-----------|---------|
| **Element** | Water (水) |
| **Yin/Yang** | Yin |
| **Category** | Southern Dipper Star (南斗星) |
| **Symbol** | Moon, night, feminine, property |
| **Keyword** | Nurturance |

## Personality Profile

Tai Yin individuals are:
- **Gentle and compassionate** — natural caregivers
- **Intuitive and perceptive** — sense what others miss
- **Romantic and artistic** — drawn to beauty and aesthetics
- **Home-oriented** — value domestic harmony and comfort
- **Emotionally deep** — rich inner life

### Strengths
- Emotional intelligence and empathy
- Aesthetic sense and artistic talent
- Financial luck through real estate and property
- Nurturing and supportive nature

### Challenges
- Mood fluctuations (like the moon's phases)
- Over-sensitivity to criticism
- Tendency toward passivity
- Difficulty making decisive choices

## Career & Wealth

### Ideal Careers
- Real estate and property management
- Interior design and architecture
- Counseling and therapy
- Arts and creative fields
- Hospitality and service industries
- Financial planning

### Financial Pattern
Tai Yin is strongly associated with real estate wealth. Unlike Wu Qu's active wealth-building, Tai Yin's wealth comes through property appreciation, inheritance, and patient accumulation. Night businesses (restaurants, entertainment) may also be favorable.

## Brightness Matters

Tai Yin's influence varies dramatically by palace position:

| Position | Brightness | Effect |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| You, Xu, Hai | **Bright (庙)** | Full moon power — excellent wealth and fame |
| Zi, Chou, Yin | **Dim (落陷)** | New moon — emotions unstable, wealth delayed |

## In the 12 Palaces

| Palace | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Life Palace** | Gentle, attractive, artistic personality |
| **Career Palace** | Success in real estate, arts, or helping professions |
| **Wealth Palace** | Excellent — especially through property |
| **Spouse Palace** | Beautiful/charming spouse; emotional marriage |
| **Property Palace** | Outstanding — property wealth is almost guaranteed |

## Key Combinations

- **Tai Yin + Tai Yang**: "Sun and Moon" — balanced masculine/feminine energy; powerful in correct positions
- **Tai Yin + Tian Tong**: Emotional comfort and artistic talent combined
- **Tai Yin + Tian Ji**: Intellectual and intuitive — excellent for strategic roles
- **Tai Yin + Tan Lang**: Romantic and sensual — strong romantic appeal
