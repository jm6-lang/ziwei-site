# Qi Sha (七杀星) — The Seven Kills Star

**七杀星** (Qi Sha, "Seven Kills") is one of the 14 main stars in Zi Wei Dou Shu. The most martial and rebellious of all stars, Qi Sha governs courage, decisive action, breaking through obstacles, and the willingness to fight for what is right.

## Essential Nature

| Attribute | Quality |
|-----------|---------|
| **Element** | Fire/Metal (火金) |
| **Yin/Yang** | Yang |
| **Category** | Northern Dipper Star (北斗星) |
| **Symbol** | Sword, general, rebellion, breakthrough |
| **Keyword** | Courage |

## Personality Profile

Qi Sha individuals are:
- **Brave and decisive** — act first, think later
- **Independent** — resist authority and control
- **Rebellious** — challenge the status quo
- **Resilient** — survive and thrive through adversity
- **Loyal** — fiercely devoted to those they protect

### Strengths
- Courage and boldness in crisis
- Ability to break through obstacles
- Independence and self-reliance
- Leadership in difficult situations

### Challenges
- Impulsiveness and hot temper
- Difficulty with authority and rules
- Tendency toward isolation
- May create enemies through bluntness

## Career & Wealth

### Ideal Careers
- Military and law enforcement
- Entrepreneurship and startups
- Surgery and emergency medicine
- Competitive sports
- Reform and activism
- Crisis management

### Financial Pattern
Qi Sha's wealth comes through risk and bold action. They are more likely than any other star type to become self-made entrepreneurs. However, they may also experience dramatic financial losses. The key is learning to channel courage into calculated risk.

## In the 12 Palaces

| Palace | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Life Palace** | Bold, independent, warrior-like personality |
| **Career Palace** | Success through courage and breaking new ground |
| **Wealth Palace** | Risk-reward pattern; big wins and losses |
| **Spouse Palace** | Passionate but combative; needs strong partner |
| **Migration Palace** | Commands respect; may face conflict abroad |

## Key Combinations

- **Qi Sha + Zi Wei**: "Emperor's Sword" — ultimate authority and power
- **Qi Sha + Po Jun**: "Double Destruction" — radical transformation; chaotic energy
- **Qi Sha + Tan Lang**: Desire meets courage — charismatic warrior
- **Qi Sha + Zi Wei + Tian Fu**: The ideal ruler — power, treasury, and military combined
- **Qi Sha + Hua Quan**: Supreme military or business authority

## The Warrior's Path

In Zi Wei Dou Shu, Qi Sha represents the archetype of the warrior who must learn that true courage is not the absence of fear, but the mastery of it. The most evolved Qi Sha individuals become protectors and reformers who change the world through bold, principled action.
