# Lian Zhen (廉贞) - The Chastity Star

**Lian Zhen** (廉贞), known as the "Chastity Star," "Clean Star," or "Justice Star," is one of the six primary stars in Zi Wei Dou Shu. It represents integrity, creativity, passion, and complex emotions.

## Basic Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Chinese Name** | 廉贞星 |
| **Meaning** | Chastity / Cleanliness / Integrity |
| **Element** | Fire |
| **Nature** | Yang (with yin tendencies) |
| **Symbol** | Justice, passion, creativity |

## Core Characteristics

People with Lian Zhen prominently placed tend to be:

### Strengths
- **Principled** - Strong sense of right and wrong
- **Creative** - Artistic and innovative thinking
- **Passionate** - Intense emotions and dedication
- **Independent** - Think and act for themselves
- **Direct** - Honest and straightforward
- **Charismatic** - Attract others with their intensity

### Challenges
- **Emotionally Complex** - Experience strong emotions
- **Stubborn** - Set in their ways of thinking
- **Impulsive** - Act before thinking
- **Self-righteous** - May judge others harshly
- **Jealous** - Can be possessive in relationships

## Lian Zhen in Different Palaces

### In the Life Palace (命宫)
Complex personality with strong convictions:
- Strong moral compass
- Creative and artistic talents
- Intense emotional life
- May face controversies

### In the Career Palace (官禄宫)
Success through passion and creativity:
- Creative industries
- Legal and justice professions
- Government and politics
- Education (especially moral/philosophical)
- Entertainment

### In the Marriage Palace (夫妻宫)
Complex relationship dynamics:
- Deep but sometimes turbulent emotions
- Need for emotional intensity
- May face relationship challenges
- Loyalty is paramount

### In the Wealth Palace (财帛宫)
Variable financial luck:
- May have ups and downs
- Creative ways of earning
- Legal/justice-related income possible

## Famous People with Lian Zhen

Lian Zhen prominent individuals often include:
- Artists and musicians
- Judges and lawyers
- Political figures
- Creative directors
- Entrepreneurs with strong principles

| Category | Examples |

| **Entertainment** | Film, music, performance |

## Star Combinations

### With Positive Stars
- **Lian Zhen + Zi Wei** → Authority with integrity
- **Lian Zhen + Tian Tong** → Creative expression
- **Lian Zhen + Wu Qu** → Determined action with purpose

### With Challenging Stars
- **Lian Zhen + Hua Xue** → May indicate legal troubles
- **Lian Zhen + Peach Blossom** → Complex romantic relationships

## The Dual Nature

Lian Zhen is known for its dual nature:

### Positive Aspects
- Clean and pure intentions
- Artistic creativity
- Passionate dedication
- Strong sense of justice

### Challenging Aspects
- Can be overly strict
- Emotional intensity
- Tendency toward conflict
- May burn out from passion

## Summary

Lian Zhen represents **integrity, creativity, and passionate intensity**. It bestows:
- Strong moral principles
- Creative and artistic abilities
- Deep emotional experiences
- Dedication to causes

Those influenced by Lian Zhen should focus on channeling their intensity constructively while learning to be more flexible and understanding with others.
