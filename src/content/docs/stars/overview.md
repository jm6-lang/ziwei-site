# The 14 Main Stars in Zi Wei Dou Shu

The 14 main stars (主星) form the foundation of any Zi Wei Dou Shu chart. Each star carries unique energy and influences different aspects of your life.

## Classification of Stars

### Primary Stars (正曜) - The 6 Major Stars
These are the core stars that form the main structure of the chart:

| Star | Chinese | Element | Nature |
|------|---------|---------|--------|
| Zi Wei | 紫微 | Earth | Imperial |
| Tian Ji | 天机 | Wood | Strategy |
| Tai Yang | 太阳 | Fire | Sunshine |
| Wu Qu | 武曲 | Metal | Military |
| Lian Zhen | 廉贞 | Fire | Integrity |
| Tian Tong | 天同 | Water | Harmony |

### Secondary Major Stars
These stars provide important context and support:

| Star | Chinese | Element | Nature |
|------|---------|---------|--------|
| Tian Yong | 天梁 | Metal | Supervisor |
| Ji Men | 巨门 | Water | Hidden |
| Liang Gan | 禄存 | Wood | Fortune |
| Tian Kong | 天空 | - | Void |
| Tao Hua | 桃花 | Water | Romance |

## The Four Transformation Stars
These special stars change based on the year, month, day, and hour:

| Transformation | Type |
|----------------|------|
| Lu (禄) | Fortune |
| Quan (权) | Authority |
| Ke (科) | Scholarly |
| Jie (忌) | Obstacle |

## How Stars Interact

The meaning of a star depends on:
1. **Which palace** it occupies
2. **What other stars** are present
3. **Whether it has support stars** (吉星) or challenging stars (煞星)
4. **Its relationship** with the Life Palace

## Key Principles

### Star Quality
- **Pure placement** (清) - Star alone with good support
- **Mixed placement** (杂) - Multiple stars competing
- **Empty placement** (空) - No major star

### Star Combinations
- Compatible stars together = synergy
- Conflicting stars = tension
- Support stars enhance positive effects
- Challenging stars add difficulties
