# How to Read a Zi Wei Dou Shu Chart

Your Zi Wei Dou Shu chart (命盘, Ming Pan) is a snapshot of the celestial configuration at your birth moment. Learning to read it reveals profound insights about your life path.

## The Basic Structure

A complete Zi Wei Dou Shu chart consists of:

### 12 Palaces (十二宫)
The chart is divided into 12 sections, each representing a life domain:
- **命宫 (Ming Gong)** - Life Palace
- **兄弟宫 (Xiong Di)** - Siblings Palace
- **财帛宫 (Cai Bo)** - Wealth Palace
- **田宅宫 (Tian Zhai)** - Property Palace
- **官禄宫 (Guan Lu)** - Career Palace
- **交友宫 (Jiao You)** - Friends Palace
- **迁移宫 (Qian Yi)** - Migration Palace
- **疾厄宫 (Ji Yi)** - Health Palace
- **财库宫 (Cai Ku)** - Treasury Palace
- **父田宫 (Fu Tian)** - Parents/Property Palace
- **福德宫 (Fu De)** - Blessings Palace
- **相貌宫 (Xiang Mao)** - Appearance Palace

### The 14 Main Stars
These primary stars are placed in various palaces based on your birth data:
- Tian Ji, Tai Yang, Wu Qu, Lian Zhen, Zi Wei, Tian Tong
- Plus additional stars that modify interpretations

### Auxiliary Stars
Many smaller stars add nuance and detail to your chart's interpretation.

## Reading Your Chart Step by Step

### Step 1: Find Your Life Palace
The Life Palace (命宫) is the most important palace. It represents:
- Your core personality
- Physical appearance
- Inner nature
- General life direction

### Step 2: Identify Your Body Palace
The Body Palace (身宫) shows:
- How you present yourself to the world
- Your external behavior
- Life experiences that shape you

### Step 3: Note Major Stars in Each Palace
For each palace, identify:
- Which major stars are present
- The quality of those stars (clean or mixed with auxiliary stars)
- Whether the palace is "empty" or "full"

### Step 4: Analyze Strengths and Challenges
Strong indicators:
- Major stars in Life Palace = strong personality
- Multiple stars in Wealth Palace = financial potential

Warning signs:
- Too many "killing stars" (煞星) = challenges
- Empty important palaces = weaknesses to develop

## Key Interpretive Principles

### 1. Star Combinations Matter
Individual stars are important, but their combinations are crucial:
- Two compatible stars together = enhanced positive effects
- Conflicting stars = internal tension

### 2. Palace Empty Spaces
An empty palace isn't necessarily bad:
- It means fewer predetermined influences
- You have more freedom in that life area
- Could indicate a challenge to develop

### 3. Life Cycles (大运/限)
Your chart shows not just your static destiny but also:
- **Da Yun (大运)** - 10-year cycles
- **Xiao Yun (小运)** - Yearly cycles
- Each period shows what life phase you're experiencing

## Practical Example

A typical beginner's reading might focus on:

1. **Life Palace + Main Star** → Core personality traits
2. **Wealth Palace** → Financial outlook
3. **Career Palace** → Career potential
4. **Current 10-year cycle** → Current life focus
