# Career Palace (官禄宫)

The **Career Palace** (官禄宫), also known as the Official Fortune Palace, represents your professional life, career achievements, academic pursuits, and public reputation.

## What the Career Palace Reveals

### Career Potential
- Natural career talents
- Professional strengths
- Achievement potential
- Leadership abilities

### Academic Luck
- Learning abilities
- Educational success
- Scholarly achievements
- Study luck

### Public Reputation
- How others see you professionally
- Social status
- Public standing
- Professional image

## Reading the Career Palace

### Strong Career Palace Indicators
With major stars and supportive stars:
- Clear career direction
- Professional recognition
- Leadership potential
- Academic success

### Challenged Career Palace Indicators
With killing stars or empty:
- Career instability
- Challenges in the workplace
- Need for more effort
- May change careers frequently

## Star Placements in Career Palace

| Star | Career Meaning |
|------|---------------|
| Zi Wei | Leadership positions, authority |
| Tian Ji | Strategic roles, consulting |
| Tai Yang | Public-facing careers, media |
| Wu Qu | Business, finance, entrepreneurship |
| Lian Zhen | Creative industries, law, government |
| Tian Tong | Service industries, hospitality |

## Career Development Tips

### Leveraging Your Strengths
1. **Identify your natural talents** - Work with your star placements
2. **Align career choice** - Match your career with your chart
3. **Time career moves** - Use luck cycles for job changes
4. **Address weaknesses** - Develop skills in weaker areas

### Career Challenges by Palace Status
- **Strong palace** - Seize opportunities for advancement
- **Challenged palace** - Work harder, be patient, consider career changes
- **Empty palace** - Flexibility, multiple career paths possible

## Career Palace and Other Palaces
