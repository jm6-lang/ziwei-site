# 迁移宫

The **Migration Palace** (迁移宫, Qian Yi Gong) governs your **life outside your home territory** -- travel, migration, foreign affairs, and how you are perceived when you are not in your familiar environment. This palace reveals what happens when you step outside your comfort zone: how others see you in unfamiliar situations, how well you adapt to new environments, and your potential for success in foreign lands or outside your home base.

In modern terms, the Migration Palace is crucial for understanding your **social image outside close circles**, your potential for international success, and your ability to thrive in competitive external environments.

**What This Palace Governs:**
- How strangers and new acquaintances perceive you
- Your experiences when traveling domestically or internationally
- Your adaptability and success in foreign environments
- Your reputation in public and social spheres
- Interactions with authorities outside your home territory
