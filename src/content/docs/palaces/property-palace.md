# Property Palace (田宅宫)

The **Property Palace** (田宅宫, Tian Zhai Gong) is one of the twelve palaces in Zi Wei Dou Shu, governing your relationship with real estate, property, home environment, and family roots. It reveals whether you will own property, the quality of your living conditions, and your connection to your ancestral home.

## Core Meanings

| Aspect | Interpretation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Primary** | Property ownership, real estate, home environment |
| **Secondary** | Ancestral heritage, family roots, living conditions |
| **Financial** | Immovable assets, land holdings, inheritance |

## Character Analysis

### Strong Property Palace
- Likely to own multiple properties
- Values home comfort and security
- Strong connection to family roots
- May inherit property from ancestors
- Interest in real estate investment

### Weak Property Palace
- May rent rather than own
- Frequently changes residence
- Less emphasis on property accumulation
- May prioritize experiences over assets

## Career Implications

The Property Palace in combination with your Career Palace indicates:

- **Real estate industry** connections if both are strong
- **Property-based wealth** potential
- **Home-based business** opportunities
- **International property** investments for certain configurations

## Wealth Pattern

In Zi Wei Dou Shu, the Property Palace works with:

- **Wealth Palace** (财帛宫) - to determine total asset potential
- **Fortune Palace** (福德宫) - for financial luck and spending habits
- **Parents Palace** (父母宫) - for inheritance and family support

## Famous Configurations

### "Double Tian Zhai"
When the Property Palace contains both Tian Fu (天府) and Tian Liang (天梁), it indicates exceptional property luck and likely multiple real estate holdings.

### "Empty Property Palace"
An empty Property Palace (no major stars) suggests rental rather than ownership, or delayed property acquisition.

## Health Connections

The Property Palace also relates to:
- Digestive system
- Skin conditions related to environment
- Allergies and sensitivities to living space
