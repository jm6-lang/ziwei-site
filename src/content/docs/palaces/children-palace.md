# 子息宫

The **Children Palace** (子息宫, Zi Xi Gong) governs your relationships with **children, your creative output, and your legacy** -- what you create or leave behind that continues beyond your lifetime. In Zi Wei Dou Shu, 'children' includes not only biological offspring but also students you teach, mentees you guide, creative works you produce, and investments you make for the future.

This palace also relates to **creativity and expression** -- the ability to bring something new into existence, whether through having children, artistic creation, business building, or intellectual contributions.

**What the Children Palace Reveals:**
- Number, gender, nature, and relationship with children
- Creative abilities and creative output
- Legacy-building potential -- what you leave behind
- Investment returns and compound growth
- Fertility and reproductive health

## Key Stars in the Children Palace

## Key Stars in the Children Palace

### Favorable Patterns:

**Tian Tong (天同) in Children Palace:**
Warm, loving relationship with children. May have multiple children. Children bring happiness and joy. Strong bond between parent and child.

**Tao Hua in Children Palace:**
Beautiful, artistic children. Possible romantic relationships with younger partners. Creative legacy through artistic output.

**Lu (禄) in Children Palace:**
Children who bring good fortune. Legacy that continues to benefit family. Strong creative output that generates ongoing returns.

**Strong stars + Lu transformation:**
Multiple children or productive creative career. Strong legacy-building potential. Creative works that continue generating value over time.

### Challenging Patterns:

**Empty Children Palace:**
May indicate fewer children or delayed parenthood. Legacy building through non-traditional means such as business, philosophy, or social impact.

**Jie (忌) in Children Palace:**
Challenges with children -- may include health issues, conflicts, or distance. Difficulty leaving lasting legacy.

**Multiple 煞星:**
Complications with children or creativity. May indicate no children or childless path. Creative potential may be suppressed.

## How to Interpret Your Children Palace

## Reading Your Children Palace

### Number of Children:
Traditional interpretations suggest:
- Strong stars without 煞星 = more children likely
- Empty palace = fewer children
- Tao Hua presence = children with artistic talents
- Lu + Tian Tong = children who achieve success and bring pride

### Children's Nature:
The stars in this palace reveal children's characteristics:
- **Yang stars (Zi Wei, Wu Qu, Tai Yang)**: Active, ambitious, outward-focused children who achieve publicly
- **Yin stars (Tian Tong, Lian Zhen, Tian Ji)**: Intellectual, artistic, home-focused children
- **Lu (Fortune)**: Children with wealth potential
- **Ke (Scholarly)**: Academically inclined children
- **Quan (Authority)**: Leadership-oriented children

### Creativity Analysis:
The Children Palace also indicates creative output:
- **Strong creative stars** here = prolific creative output
- **Tao Hua** presence = artistic creativity
- **Tian Ji** presence = intellectual creativity
- **Empty** palace = may struggle with creative projects but can develop through practice
