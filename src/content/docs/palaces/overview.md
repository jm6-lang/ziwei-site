# The 12 Palaces in Zi Wei Dou Shu

The 12 palaces (十二宫) form the structural framework of a Zi Wei Dou Shu chart. Each palace represents a specific aspect of life and contains stars that influence that life area.

## The Complete System

Unlike Western astrology with its 12 signs, the Zi Wei Dou Shu palaces represent **life domains** rather than personality types. Where you are born determines your Life Palace, but the palaces remain fixed throughout your chart.

## The 12 Palaces Explained

| Palace | Chinese | English | Life Domain |
|--------|---------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | 命宫 | Life Palace | Core identity, personality |
| 2 | 兄弟宫 | Siblings Palace | Siblings, peers, resources |
| 3 | 财帛宫 | Wealth Palace | Money, finances, assets |
| 4 | 田宅宫 | Property Palace | Home, property, family roots |
| 5 | 官禄宫 | Career Palace | Career,学业, achievements |
| 6 | 交友宫 | Friends Palace | Relationships, social circle |
| 7 | 迁移宫 | Migration Palace | Travel, external luck |
| 8 | 疾厄宫 | Health Palace | Health, wellbeing |
| 9 | 财库宫 | Treasury Palace | Savings, accumulated wealth |
| 10 | 父田宫 | Parents Palace | Parents, inheritance |
| 11 | 福德宫 | Blessings Palace | Happiness, 福气 |
| 12 | 相貌宫 | Appearance Palace | Appearance, reputation |

## How to Read Palaces

### Palace Strength
Each palace can be:
- **Strong (旺)** - Favorable stars present
- **Neutral (平)** - Average star placement
- **Weak (弱)** - Challenging stars or empty

### Star Content
A palace is analyzed by:
1. **Major stars** present
2. **Auxiliary stars** present
3. **Empty vs. Full** status
4. **Transformation stars**

## Key Palaces to Focus On

### Life Palace (命宫)
The most important palace - your core personality:
- Physical appearance
- Inner nature
- Life direction
- Overall life luck

### Body Palace (身宫)
Your external presentation:
- How others perceive you
- Life experiences that shape you
- External behavior patterns

### Wealth Palace (财帛宫)
Financial matters:
- Earning ability
- Financial luck
- Money management

### Career Palace (官禄宫)
Professional life:
- Career success
- Academic achievement
- Professional reputation

## Palace Interactions

Palaces influence each other:

| Relationship | Effect |
|--------------|--------|
| Life Palace → Wealth Palace | Personality affects finances |
| Life Palace → Career Palace | Personality affects career |
| Parents → Property | Family affects home/property |
