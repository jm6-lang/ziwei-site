# Wealth Palace (иіҐеЄЫеЃЂ)

The **Wealth Palace** (иіҐеЄЫеЃЂ) is one of the most scrutinized palaces in Zi Wei Dou Shu, as it directly relates to your financial luck, earning ability, and attitude toward money.

## What the Wealth Palace Reveals

### Financial Luck
- How money flows in your life
- Earning potential
- Financial luck cycles
- Investment abilities

### Money Attitude
- Your relationship with wealth
- Spending habits
- Financial values
- Risk tolerance

## Reading the Wealth Palace

### Strong Wealth Palace Indicators
With major stars and supportive stars:
- Good earning ability
- Financial stability
- Smart money management
- Investment luck

### Challenged Wealth Palace Indicators
With killing stars or empty:
- Financial struggles
- Irregular income
- Money management issues
- Need to work harder

## Star Placements in Wealth Palace

| Star | Financial Meaning |
|------|-------------------|
| Wu Qu | Strong earning, business success |
| Zi Wei | Wealth through authority/position |
| Tai Yang | Public income, recognition wealth |
| Tian Tong | Variable luck, need to save |
| Lian Zhen | Creative income, legal money |
| Tian Ji | Strategic investments |

## Wealth Palace Tips

### Enhancing Financial Luck
1. **Work with your stars** - Leverage your natural abilities
2. **Time your moves** - Use luck cycles for major financial decisions
3. **Address weaknesses** - Learn from challenged periods
4. **Balance accumulation and spending** - Neither extreme is ideal

### Common Wealth Palace Challenges
- **Empty palace** - May need multiple income sources
- **Killing stars** - Be cautious with investments
- **Variable stars** - Income may fluctuate

## Wealth vs. Treasury Palace

| Palace | Focus |

| Treasury Palace (иіҐеЇУеЃЂ) | Savings, accumulated wealth |

## Summary

The Wealth Palace shows your **financial destiny**. Understanding it helps you:
- Make informed financial decisions
- Time investments wisely
- Leverage your natural talents
- Prepare for challenging periods
