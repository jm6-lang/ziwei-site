# 仆役宫

The **Servants Palace** (仆役宫, Pu Yi Gong) governs your relationships with **subordinates, employees, helpers, and the broader social network** that supports your life. In modern terms, this palace reveals how you relate to those who work for you or with you in less-than-equal positions, and how effectively you can mobilize social support.

This palace also governs **contractual relationships, legal agreements, and organized group affiliations** -- the formal and informal networks that extend your personal influence.

**What the Servants Palace Reveals:**
- How you manage and are managed by subordinates
- Quality and reliability of your social support system
- Helper relationships and support networks
- Membership in clubs, organizations, associations
- Success in agreements, contracts, and legal deals

## Key Stars in the Servants Palace

## Key Stars in the Servants Palace

### Favorable Patterns:

**Zuo Fu + You Bi in Servants Palace:**
Excellent relationships with subordinates and social network. Loyal helpers and supportive friendships. People naturally gravitate to help and support.

**Lu (禄) in Servants Palace:**
Financial benefits through subordinates and social network. Loyalty brings profit. Contracts and agreements tend to be favorable.

**Strong stars + Lu:**
Large social network with quality connections. Ability to mobilize people and resources effectively. Strong team loyalty.

### Challenging Patterns:

**Multiple 煞星:**
Unreliable subordinates, social network challenges, contractual disputes. Difficulty building and maintaining social support.

**Jie (忌) in Servants Palace:**
Betrayal by subordinates or trusted helpers. Legal conflicts over contracts. Struggles managing employees. May be stabbed in the back by those closest in work context.

**Ji Men in Servants Palace:**
Complex relationships with subordinates, possible hidden agendas in social network. Cannot fully trust those who work for them.

## Reading Your Servants Palace

### Leadership Analysis:
This palace reveals leadership style:

**Strong indicators:**
- Natural leader who commands respect from subordinates
- Employees are loyal and productive
- Good at delegation and team building
- Social network actively supports goals
- Contracts and agreements tend to work out well

**Challenging indicators:**
- Difficulty managing subordinates
- Social network does not provide support
- Tendency to micromanage or distrust
- Team-building challenges
- Contracts frequently cause problems

### Social Network Quality:
- **Good stars**: Quality friendships, reliable social support
- **Challenged stars**: Fair-weather friends, network unreliable in crisis
- **Empty palace**: Smaller network, more independent path
- **Ji Men present**: Network exists but may have hidden agendas
