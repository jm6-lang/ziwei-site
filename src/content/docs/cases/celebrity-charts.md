# Celebrity Charts Analysis

Learning from real-life examples is one of the best ways to understand Zi Wei Dou Shu. Let's analyze the charts of famous personalities.

## Why Study Celebrity Charts?

1. **Learn interpretation** - See how theory applies in practice
2. **Understand patterns** - Recognize recurring themes
3. **Validate principles** - Confirm traditional teachings
4. **Gain insights** - Apply lessons to your own chart

## Analysis Framework

When analyzing any chart, we look at:

### 1. Life Palace
- Core personality traits
- Main stars present
- Overall character

### 2. Major Star Placements
- Key stars and their meanings
- Star combinations
- Strengths and challenges

### 3. Career Indicators
- Career Palace analysis
- Public reputation
- Achievement potential

### 4. Wealth Patterns
- Financial luck
- Wealth Palace analysis
- Money management style

## How to Apply This

### Self-Analysis Steps

1. **Identify your major stars** - Note which are prominent in your chart
2. **Find similar patterns** - Look for celebrities with similar placements
3. **Study their achievements** - Learn from their paths
4. **Apply lessons** - Use insights for your own life
