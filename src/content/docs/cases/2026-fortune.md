# 2026 Fortune Analysis

## Understanding Timing in Zi Wei Dou Shu

Zi Wei Dou Shu isn't just about your static chart—it's also about **timing**. Your luck cycles through different periods that affect every aspect of your life.

## The Luck Cycle System

### Major Luck Cycles (大运/Da Yun)
- Duration: 10 years per cycle
- Age range: Typically starts from birth
- Effects: Major life themes and opportunities

### Minor Luck Cycles (小运/Xiao Yun)
- Duration: 1 year per cycle
- Effects: Annual themes and events

### Yearly Stars (流年星/Liu Nian Xing)
- Change annually
- Affect the specific year's fortune
- Based on the Chinese zodiac year
